About Me

Hello! I'm Sumeya Hassen, a passionate software engineering student and aspiring tech entrepreneur from Ethiopia. Currently in my third year at Bahir Dar University, I am deeply engaged in software development, frontend technologies, and mobile app development.

I have a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Flutter, Python, and databases, and I’m continuously expanding my skills in AI tools, Android development, and full-stack development. My goal is to create technology-driven solutions that make a difference, and I am always excited to collaborate, learn, and explore new opportunities in the tech industry.

About Derash

Derash is an innovative mobile application and website project aimed at transforming public transportation in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. The platform is designed to enhance passenger safety, prevent theft, and improve efficiency for daily commuters.

Derash provides real-time public transport updates, allowing users to track buses, report safety concerns, and access emergency support. The platform integrates AI-driven security features to protect women and all passengers from attacks, ensuring a safer commuting experience.

My vision for Derash is to scale it beyond Addis Ababa, expanding to other cities globally with similar transportation challenges.

About Agelgel

Agelgel is a revolutionary agricultural product delivery system designed to connect farmers directly with customers in Ethiopia. The goal is to streamline agricultural trade, reducing the role of middlemen and ensuring fair prices for both farmers and consumers.

The platform operates through SMS, mobile app, and website, allowing customers to browse fresh produce and place orders while farmers update product availability. By leveraging local networks and offline solutions, Agelgel makes agricultural trade more accessible, transparent, and efficient.

My vision for Agelgel is to create a sustainable agricultural ecosystem that empowers farmers, reduces food waste, and improves market efficiency.
